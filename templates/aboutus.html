Our Vision

Our vision is to create a future where every student, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to thrive academically and personally. We envision a world where learning is an exciting and transformative journey, and where each student discovers their unique potential. Through our dedicated tutoring and mentorship, we aspire to ignite a lifelong love for learning and empower individuals to achieve their dreams and make a positive impact on society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional tutoring and guidance that caters to the diverse needs of every student we serve. We are committed to fostering a supportive learning environment where students can excel academically, build confidence, and develop essential skills. Our team of dedicated educators is passionate about tailoring our approach to each student's unique learning style, helping them reach their academic and personal goals.

Our Beliefs


At Home Tutoring.com, we believe that every student deserves a personal tutor, and we are dedicated to promoting equity, opportunity, and achievement for all learners. To that end, we partner with colleges and universities, K–12 schools and districts, public and state libraries, employee benefits programs, 1-to-1 tutoring and homework help in more than 250 subjects.


Over more than two decades of supporting students, educators, school leaders, and families, we have helped institutions increase student pass and persistence rates, and learners become more confident in their schoolwork. Our learner satisfaction rates remain consistently high: 97 percent of post-session survey respondents would recommend Tutor.com to a friend, and 98 percent are glad their institution offers Tutor.com. The feeling is mutual. We are honored to partner with institutions and organizations to help them scale the human connection and provide individualized support—anytime, anywhere.


Our Story


In 1998, a small group of passionate education and tech professionals had a terrific URL and a big idea—use the Internet to connect students to tutors whenever and wherever they needed help. So, they recruited about a hundred tutors and created one of the first online, interactive classrooms.


In 2000, Home Tutoring.com, Inc. was incorporated and began partnering with institutions to ensure that students from all socioeconomic backgrounds would have access to highly effective 1-to-1 tutoring and homework help, day or night, from any Internet-enabled device.


In 2014, Home Tutoring.com acquired The Princeton Review®, a world leader in education services, and added The Princeton Review test preparation services to its online tutoring platform and offerings.

Our Values


Our work is guided by a set of core values that define who we are and how we operate. These values include:

  • **Excellence:** We are committed to providing the highest quality tutoring and support services.
  • **Equity:** We believe in equal access to educational opportunities for all students.
  • **Innovation:** We continuously seek new and better ways to enhance the learning experience.
  • **Integrity:** We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our interactions.
  • **Collaboration:** We work closely with students, parents, educators, and institutions to achieve shared goals.
  • **Passion:** We are passionate about education and dedicated to helping students succeed.

Our Team


Our team of tutors and educators is the heart of Home Tutoring.com. Each tutor is carefully selected, not only for their subject matter expertise but also for their ability to connect with students and inspire a love for learning. Our tutors come from diverse backgrounds and bring a wealth of experience to the online classroom. They are passionate about teaching and are committed to providing personalized support to help each student reach their full potential.


We invest in ongoing professional development for our tutors to ensure they are equipped with the latest teaching strategies and technologies. This commitment to excellence in our team translates directly into a better learning experience for our students.


Partner With Us
